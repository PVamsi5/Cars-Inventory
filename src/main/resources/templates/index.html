List of api's present in the inventory

  • "/" --- (home page)
  • "/cars" ---
    • GET Request (to get all the cars)
    • Post Request (to add specific car in inventory)
  • "/cars/id" ---
    • GET Request --- (to get car with specific id)
    • PUT Request --- (to update car with specific id)
    • DELETE Request --- (to delete car with specific id)
    • PATCH Request --- (updates the given details with id)
